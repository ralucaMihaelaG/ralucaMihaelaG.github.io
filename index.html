About us

Who?

There are souls in this world who have the gift of finding joy everywhere, and leaving it behind them when they go. It's a shame though we can't actually wrap and offer joy. So when you find yourself in need of something a bit more physical, GIFTER will come up with the best ideas!

Why?

Maybe you got a last-minute invitation to a party? Or forgot one of your friends have their birthday this week? You get constrained by time to find a gift fast, and some flowers or money won't do the trick - you need something more thoughtful than that.

How?

Gifter is a platform for gift recommendations, based on the personality traits, hobbies and interests of the receiver. You can provide us details about their friend/coworker/family member, then you choose from the resulted suggestions.
